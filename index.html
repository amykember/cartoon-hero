About Jack-Jack

Jack-Jack He belongs to a family of superheroes, but his own powers were only recently discovered. While his siblings each have one power, Jack-Jack has many. He can shapeshift, crawl on walls, levitate, or transform into a monster. He doesn't have complete control over his abilities yet, but with the help of a new suit crafted by Edna Mode, he is learning to fulfill his potential.
